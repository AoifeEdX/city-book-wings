About Us

CityBookWings was created for those who love reading and travel. We created this app with all the travel lovers in mind. We hope you enjoy it and find your dream destination and a new favorite book.

Whether you're planning a trip or simply interested in books about a specific location, CityBookWings has you covered.

What is CityBookWings?

CityBookWings provides flight warningrmation for any city you'd like to travel to, and recommends books about that city that you might like to read.
